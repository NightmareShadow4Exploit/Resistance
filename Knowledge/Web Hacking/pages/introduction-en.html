    In this introduction, we will explore how you can begin your journey into the world of Website Hacking.

    If you search for "website hacking" on the internet, you’ll find plenty of resources. But, let me be clear — that information is only the tip of the iceberg.

    From my personal experience, hacking websites effectively requires a deep understanding of the various types of attacks that can be carried out against them.

    Web administrators work tirelessly to secure their sites, but as hackers, we must always stay ahead by learning all possible attack methods.


    Among the many types of attacks, the OWASP Top 10 stands out as the most critical. These attacks are the most dangerous, posing significant threats to a website's data.

    However, I’ve observed a common pitfall: Since the OWASP Top 10 is widely recognized, many website owners prioritize addressing these vulnerabilities first. But, in doing so, they may overlook other potential weaknesses, ultimately leaving their sites exposed to compromise.
